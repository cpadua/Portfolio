Adobe Premiere vs. Sony Vegas

Adobe Premiere and Sony Vegas are the two most popular video editing software out there and are important to know about if you're new to web development.


If you're new to video editing, then you may hear that many video editors use Sony Vegas Pro or Adobe Premiere. I'm not going to make a long, boring article like everyone else. This website will be quick and to the point about showing you all of the info you'll need to know about Premiere and Vegas before you make the decision to buy either software.





Before you read the list, here some background information about Sony Vegas and Premiere Pro.

Sony Vegas

Sony Vegas was originally an audio software, named SoundForge, that was made by a company named Sonic Foundry. One day, Sony bought the company and turned SoundForge into a full-fledged video-editing software. Sony Vegas has an easy-to-use interface and still maintains a well-designed functionality.

Adobe Premiere

Adobe Premiere is a video-editing software that can be used with other Adobe applications. Premiere is more complex than Sony Vegas, but it does support more formats for more audio and video, and provides more publishing presets.
